    • The Story Behind Marotzki Bookstore

    A tradition of excellence, love of books and dedicated service - a bookstore for students in Israel that believes in the power of knowledge

    Marotzki is not just a bookstore - it's a cultural institution with a rich tradition and deep love for the world of books. Our story began many years ago, when our founders decided to create a place where books are not only sold, but also celebrated and honored. They wanted to create a bookstore that would be a second home for passionate readers, a place where you can spend hours among the shelves, discover new stories and feel the magic of literature.

    From the beginning, our vision was clear: to make knowledge and culture accessible to everyone. We believe that every person deserves access to quality books, regardless of their financial situation. Therefore, we specialize in providing academic textbooks at affordable prices for students, and offer a wide variety of books in all categories at fair and worthwhile prices.

    The unique design of our store reflects our love for the tradition and history of classic bookstores. The vintage style creates a pleasant and inviting atmosphere, reminiscent of the charm of traditional bookstores from the past. Every corner of the store is carefully designed to create a unique experience that encourages staying, discovering and falling in love with books again.

    The Values That Guide Us

    Love of Books

    We believe in the life-changing power of books. Every book is a gateway to a new world, and our mission is to help every person find the book that will change their life.

    Dedicated Service

    Our team consists of passionate readers who love what they do. We are here to help, advise and ensure that every customer finds exactly what they are looking for.

    Honesty and Transparency

    We believe in fair prices and complete transparency. You won't find hidden costs or inflated prices with us - just fair prices and honest service.

    Community and Tradition

    Marotzki Bookstore is more than a business place - it's part of the community. We are proud of the relationships we have built with our customers over the years.

    Our Mission

    The mission of Marotzki Bookstore is simple yet important: to make knowledge and culture accessible to everyone. We believe that books are not luxuries - they are a basic need essential for personal development, education and growth. Therefore, we make every effort to offer a wide variety of books at affordable prices, especially for students and parents who invest in their children's education.

    We are also committed to preserving the tradition of classic bookstores - places where you can spend time, discover new books and receive personal and professional advice. In an era of online shopping and digital books, we believe there is tremendous value in the physical experience of visiting a real bookstore, with shelves full of possibilities and people who love books.

    Additionally, we see ourselves as partners in the educational and cultural process of the community. We work in collaboration with educational institutions, teachers and parents to ensure that we always have the right books at the right price. We believe that investing in books is investing in the future.

    Our Team

    The Marotzki team consists of passionate and professional readers who love the world of books and are committed to providing excellent service. Each team member brings deep knowledge in the field of literature and specialization in different categories, so there will always be someone who can help you find the perfect book.

    Literature Experts

    Our team reads extensively and knows the world of books in depth - from classics to contemporary novels. We are always updated on the newest and most fascinating books.

    Academic Specialists

    We have experts who know in depth the requirements of every educational institution and can help students find the textbooks they need for every course.

    Children's Book Advisors

    Team members who specialize in children's literature and can advise parents which books are suitable for their children's age and level.

    Get to Know Us Better

    We would be happy to see you at Marotzki Bookstore and share with you the magic of the world of books. Our team awaits you with a warm welcome.
